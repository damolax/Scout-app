Scouting Queue
Correct workflow: the extension downloads a raw business list. Upload that file here. Scout shows one business at a time, opens its profile link, helps you scout the page/reviews, drafts a careful non-salesy message, and removes the business from the pending queue only after the message is sent/marked sent.

Message rule: Scout never claims a problem unless it found evidence. Low-evidence leads get a respectful process question instead of a fake pain claim.
Opening framework: Evidence → humility → systems question → light permission. The goal is a reply, not a pitch.
Preferred queue upload: Business, Rating, Reviews, Band, Profile link. Completed scouting export for Elevate uses: Scouter ID, Scouter Name, Business, Industry, Email, Match Score, Issues Found, Status, Message Type, Message Sent, Their Reply, Source, Platform, Scouted At. Important: Elevate login is the final source of truth. A normal scouter can only upload scouting for their own signed-in Elevate ID.

How it works: hosted, the app uses a free, keyless AI (Pollinations) — no sign-up and no pop-up. Add your Gemini key below and it kicks in automatically when the free one is busy, so you can analyze hundreds a day without hitting a wall. Inside Claude's preview, AI calls are blocked — host the file and it works. Needs you online; offline it falls back to your library. Honest note: the free AI runs through a third-party service, so message text passes through their servers — fine for outreach, don't paste anything sensitive.
