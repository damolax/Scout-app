# Scout v10.10 — Harder Scouting Level

- Made Scouting Level much harder, especially from Strategist upward.
- Users can now see the full list of 12 levels, but exact next-stage requirements stay hidden.
- Added hints instead of exact requirements.
- Real replies and replies sent from Scout now have the biggest impact.
- Easy actions still help, but they are capped so they cannot push someone too high too fast.
- Later levels are intentionally long-term. Ultimate is designed to be extremely hard.

No SQL is required.
