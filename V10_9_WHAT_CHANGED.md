# Scout v10.9 — Real Replies, Locked Sender Replies, Dashboard Order, Scouting Level

- Improved real-reply classification so human replies like “we do not need this,” “thank you for sharing,” “send more details,” and direct criticisms count as real replies.
- Kept strong auto/ticket/receipt messages out of real replies.
- Added SQL cleanup to repair old reply rows.
- Replying to a business is now locked to the Gmail account that sent that business the original message.
- Replies page now has a Read button so you can see the exact prospect message before responding.
- Business conversation timeline now has a Read button for exact sent/inbound message text.
- Dashboard analysis comes before the setup checklist.
- Time-zone strip is hidden under “Check best sending time.”
- Added Scouting Level, a small fun progress bubble with 12 stages ending at Ultimate.
- Scouting Level points are earned from existing Scout activities: trusted emails, Auto Scout checks, sent messages, real replies, replies sent from Scout, connected Gmail accounts, templates, schedules, and app activity.
