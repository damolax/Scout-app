# Scout v10.18 — Time-Zone Language Reply Classifier

- Clarifies that Scout does not use a paid translation API.
- Adds stronger language coverage for the countries in the send-time section:
  - US / US West / US East: English
  - Canada: English + French
  - Germany: German
  - France: French
  - Spain: Spanish
- Adds extra Italian and Dutch auto-reply patterns for European outreach.
- Keeps clear human replies counted as real replies.
- Stops common multilingual ticket confirmations, received-message receipts, out-of-office emails, and feedback requests from counting as real replies.
