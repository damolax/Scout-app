SCOUT v10.38.9 — SIMPLIFIED REPLIES PAGE

CORRECTED
- Removed the broken /operations "Sync replies" link.
- Removed unused inbox-sync controls and client sync code from the Google send-only verification page.
- Reply-history queries now include raw message data so the Read modal has the best available full-message fallback.
- Database and permission errors are displayed visibly with a retry button.
- Export is accurately named "Export visible" and exports the currently filtered rows only.
- Business names and business emails are loaded and displayed instead of a generic "Open" label.
- Delivery problems use both stored reply signals and no-inbox records without claiming that a partial page sample is a complete total.

SIMPLIFIED
- The main page now prioritizes real prospect replies.
- Added one search field and three compact filters: message type, template, and sender.
- The primary table combines business, message preview, type, template, sender, received time, Read, and Open & reply.
- Automatic messages, delivery problems, and unmatched signals are moved into three closed sections.
- Removed the two long sample-based performance tables because their 1,000/150-row limits could mislead users.

UNCHANGED SAFETY
- Gmail inbox scanning remains disabled in this Google send-only verification build.
- Follow-up sending remains disabled at the page, API, and worker levels.
- No new Supabase migration is required for this page-only update.
