# How to Use Scout

Scout finds leads, sends emails, watches replies, and helps you follow up.

The simple rule: if you want Scout to send emails, keep Scout open.

## Home
- Today numbers: shows what happened today.
- Download today report: downloads today's numbers.
- Bell: opens Scout alerts.
- Live Work: shows what Scout is doing right now.

## Find Leads
- Upload CSV: add your lead list.
- Auto Scout: finds emails for leads without emails.
- Verify emails: check and repair leads before sending.
- Leads list: shows your businesses and contacts.
- Country filter: uses countries found in your uploaded leads.
- Bad inboxes: shows bounced, blocked, and no-inbox emails.

## Send Emails
- Ready to send: leads Scout can contact now.
- Connected senders: Gmail accounts ready to send.
- Due follow-ups: people who are due after 72 hours with no real reply.
- Audience: the lead group.
- Location from uploaded list: country from your uploaded leads.
- Template category: email type.
- How many: number to send.
- Delay between emails: wait time between emails.
- Template: use one template or rotate templates.
- Sender: use one Gmail or rotate Gmail senders.
- Max from this sender: maximum from a Gmail account for this run.
- Send Now: sends immediately while Scout is open.
- Refresh: reloads the page data.
- More options: test-only mode, fix lead status, check bad inboxes, and download results.
- Ready Leads: show exact leads if you want to select them.
- Preview: show the email before sending.
- Send Due Follow-ups Now: sends follow-up emails now.
- Schedule Email: saves a first-email send for later.
- Run Due Sends Now: starts saved sends whose time has arrived.
- Add phone reminder: adds a phone/calendar reminder.

## Replies
- Real replies: replies from real people.
- Auto replies: automatic messages.
- No inbox / blocked: bad inboxes or failed emails.
- Conversation: shows what was sent and what came back.

## Templates
- Initial template: first email only.
- Follow-up template: after 72 hours with no real reply.
- Reply template: only for replying to a person.
- Subject: email title.
- Body: email message.
- Fields like {name}: Scout replaces them with lead details.

## Settings
- Connect Gmail: add a Gmail sender.
- Daily safe limit: maximum per day.
- Default max/run: normal maximum per run.
- Pause: stop one Gmail from sending.
- Upload logo: add your logo.
- Save signature & logo: save inside Scout.
- Save + sync to Gmail: save inside Gmail too.
- App Health Check: check if Scout is ready.

## Best daily flow
1. Upload or find leads.
2. Run Auto Scout for leads without emails.
3. Go to Send Emails.
4. Pick audience, country, template, sender, and count.
5. Click Send Now.
6. Check Replies later.
7. After 72 hours, go to Due Follow-ups and click Send Due Follow-ups Now.
