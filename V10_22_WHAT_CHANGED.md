# Scout v10.22 — Free Mailbox Auto Scout Priority

Auto Scout now handles Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail, Outlook, iCloud, Proton and similar free mailboxes more intelligently.

## Rules

- Business-domain emails are still best when available.
- Gmail/Yahoo/Outlook-style emails are accepted when Scout sees them on the official business website, contact page, footer, or source evidence.
- Free mailboxes found only in code, fake examples, placeholders, or unrelated pages are kept out of Ready leads.
- Auto Scout uses clearer reasons so users know when a free mailbox was accepted as the business contact email.

## Why

Many small stores use Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail, iCloud, or ISP mailboxes as their real business inbox. Scout should not reject those just because they are not on the business domain.
