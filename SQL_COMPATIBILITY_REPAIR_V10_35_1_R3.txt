Scout v10.35.1 Scale Guard R3 database compatibility repair

R3 preserves the R2 seed_inbox_tests compatibility work and additionally handles
historical sent_messages rows whose gmail_account_id points to a Gmail account that
was later disconnected or deleted.

The active-account lifetime summary now joins against gmail_accounts and skips only
those orphaned references. It does not delete or rewrite the historical sent_messages
rows. The lifetime-stat trigger also verifies the active Gmail account/workspace pair
before updating the derived summary table.
